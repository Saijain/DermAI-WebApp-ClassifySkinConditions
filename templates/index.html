About DermAI

IMPORTANT: DermAI does NOT provide medical diagnoses.

DermAI is an AI-powered skin health analysis tool designed to encourage proactive dermatological care. Many people overlook or delay addressing skin concerns, often because they're unsure if a condition warrants professional attention. DermAI helps bridge this gap by providing preliminary insights that encourage you to seek professional evaluation.

Encouraging Professional Care: DermAI is specifically designed to encourage more frequent dermatologist visits and help identify skin problems that might otherwise go unchecked. By providing accessible, preliminary information, we aim to reduce barriers to seeking professional care and help you make informed decisions about when to see a dermatologist. Early detection and professional evaluation are crucial for effective treatment of many skin conditions.

Important: This tool is for informational purposes only and should never replace professional medical advice. DermAI does NOT diagnose, replace medical evaluation, provide treatment, or guarantee accuracy. Always consult with a qualified dermatologist for proper diagnosis and treatment, especially for serious or persistent skin concerns.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use good lighting - natural light works best
  • Ensure the skin area is in focus and clearly visible
  • Remove any filters or effects from the image
  • Take the photo from a reasonable distance (not too close, not too far)
  • Make sure the image file is under 16MB
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP
